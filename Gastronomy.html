The Art of Pairing

Discover the perfect marriage of flavor and tradition

Classic Pairings

From the creamy Brie to the sharp Roquefort, explore the exquisite combinations of France's finest wines and cheeses. Each pairing tells a story of its region and tradition.

Brie & Chardonnay

The buttery, oaky notes of a classic Chardonnay beautifully complement the rich, creamy texture of a ripe Brie. A harmonious pairing from the heart of France.

Roquefort & Sauternes

A truly divine contrast. The intense saltiness of Roquefort blue cheese is perfectly balanced by the honeyed sweetness of a Sauternes dessert wine.

Goat Cheese & Sauvignon Blanc

The crisp, grassy, and citrus notes of a Loire Valley Sauvignon Blanc cut through the tangy creaminess of fresh Chèvre goat cheese.

Comté & Pinot Noir

The nutty, slightly sweet flavor of aged Comté is a perfect match for the earthy, red fruit notes of a Burgundian Pinot Noir.

Camembert & Beaujolais

The light, fruity character of a Gamay grape from Beaujolais complements the earthy, mushroomy flavors of a soft, ripe Camembert.

Munster & Gewürztraminer

An Alsatian classic. The aromatic, off-dry Gewürztraminer stands up to the pungent, powerful aroma of Munster cheese with perfect balance.
